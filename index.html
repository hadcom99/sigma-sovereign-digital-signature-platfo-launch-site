Comment ça marche

Quatre étapes. Un PDF court-admissible.

1

Upload your contract

Drag your PDF directly into SigMa or push it automatically from SAP, Oracle, or Odoo via the pre-built connector. The document is instantly encrypted and stored exclusively on Algerian sovereign infrastructure.

2

Define signatories and order

Assign up to 10 signatories, set the signing sequence, and add conditional routing rules — all in a bilingual Arabic/French interface your team already knows. No IT ticket required.

3

Signatories authenticate and sign

Each party authenticates via their AECE hardware token, generating a Class 3 qualified signature secured by HSM key custody. The entire sequence completes in an average of 8.4 minutes.

4

Receive a court-admissible sealed PDF

SigMa automatically assembles the tamper-evident evidence package — signatures, timestamps, audit trail, and AECE accreditation certificate — into a single sealed PDF admissible as legal proof under Algerian law.
